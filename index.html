About Me

Hi, my name is Michael Pfeifer. I’m currently a senior at Owatonna High School. I live on a farm, and am the oldest of 4 boys. In school, I really like math, and also like playing my trumpet in the concert band. The extra-curricular activities I’m in are jazz band, marching band, and pep band. Outside of band, I was in the Pulse, the video group. I was also in Game Design Club, a club that teaches kids what it is like behind the scenes of game design. I am also the lead programmer in robotics. At home, (after homework and trumpet practice) much of my time is dedicated to my rubik's cubes. My collection is up to 17 cubes (among other shapes) or more. My record is 36 seconds (minus my friend's reaction time)!

After high school, I will be attending SDSMT, the South Dakota School of Mines & Technology. I will major in the field of computer science (either information systems or data security).
