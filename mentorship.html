Mentorship

During my senior year in the high school's "Options" program, I wanted to have a mentorship in the computer science field. After some digging, we found out that Federated Insurance was willing to teach me! I met with Steve Thein, who was able to set up several interviews (19!) with various employees working in IS at Federated.

I learned about every branch of CS. I really enjoyed learning about networking, security, and application development. I also learned quite a bit about database administration, project management, web development, and even learned about their help desk. I also found it interesting to see how many languages there were in place in Federated's massive system. I think I counted 10 languages, a different one for almost every two branches.

During this mentorship, I even learned enough to choose what college to go to, and what to major in! It was a great experience for me, and I owe a huge thank you to Federated, and all the employees who took their time to meet with me!
