Speedcubing

My favorite hobby has always been solving Rubik's Cubes. It took me 7 years after I first solved a cube (in 6th grade) to decide I wanted to solve it faster. During my senior year, I started an inquiry through the options program on speedsolving the Rubik's cube. Not only did I learn a completely new set of algorithms, I discovered a community of people like myself at reddit.com/r/cubers, and even learned how to improve myself. I learned how to learn. Please check out my final presentation and the resources I used to learn!
